About

Hi! I'm Dmitriy, street photographer based in Moscow, Russia. I started my journey into the world of street photography in 2021. For me Street Photography is the art of documenting the human experience in public spaces. I photograph streetlife as a means to preserve images of society the way I see it unfolding infront of me. Sometimes beautiful, sometimes ugly. Sometimes personal, sometimes distant. A good street photograph for me has to be honest and not designed. I want to witness and capture authentic expressions of human life. To me, this quote by Elliott Erwitt sums it up nicely: “Photography is simply a function of noticing things.” On this page you can find collections of street photographs from 2017 up until today.
